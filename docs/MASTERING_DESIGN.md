# GOD REALM MASTERING - DESIGN SPECIFICATION

## Aesthetic: Midnight Ember / Celestial Forge
The mastering section is the "Heart of the Sun" within the God Realm. It features a heavy, etched stone background with high-energy solar visualizations and glowing glass interfaces.

## 🎨 Color Palette
- **Obsidian Stone**: `hsl(0, 0%, 4%)` - Deep black background with stone grain.
- **Solar Flare**: `hsl(25, 100%, 50%)` - Primary glow (#ff6600).
- **Celestial Gold**: `hsl(40, 80%, 45%)` - Parameter readouts and high-lights.
- **Ether Grey**: `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.05)` - Subtle UI boundaries.

## 🏗️ Layout
### 1. Central Sun Disk (Main Output)
- **Visual**: A massive circular meter with a solar corona effect.
- **Metering**: Dual-arc display for Peak and RMS.
- **Status**: Central "ACTIVE" gem that glows brighter based on gain reduction.

### 2. Four Anchor Orbits (Spectral Balance)
- Four orbital quadrants around the Sun Disk:
  - **BODY (20-200Hz)**: Top-Left orbit.
  - **SOUL (200-2k)**: Bottom-Left orbit.
  - **AIR (2k-10k)**: Top-Right orbit.
  - **SILK (10k-20k)**: Bottom-Right orbit.
- Each orbit pulses with the energy level of its respective frequency band.

### 3. Aether Saturation Grid (Center Bottom)
- **Visual**: A dark grid containing multiple intersecting "laser" lines.
- **Function**: Shows the transfer curve of the Velvet Saturation engine.
- **Dynamic**: Lines bend and glow more intensely as 'Drive' and 'Tone' increase.

### 4. Precision Rails (Side Controls)
- **Left Rail**: Input Gain, Master Drive, Color Tilt (Warm -> Bright), Low-end Dynamics.
- **Right Rail**: Cold Extension, High-end Dynamics, Stereo Width/Imager.

## ✨ Components
- **God-Knob v2**: Large glass knobs with internal solar glows and circular value rings.
- **Glyph Panel**: SVG background layer featuring ancient "Forge" glyphs etched into the obsidian.

## 🔄 Animations
- **Sun Pulse**: The solar corona scales slightly with output transients.
- **Orbit Flow**: Four Anchor rings fill/drain with a fluid motion.
- **Laser Shift**: Saturation lines interpolate smoothly between linear and non-linear states.
